We booked through a travel agent — this was 2006, before booking sites were truly reliable for international flights.

Getting from our city to Madrid required an overnight layover in Zürich, which we turned into a feature by booking a hotel near the airport and having a very good fondue dinner.

## What we packed

The key items that turned out to matter most:

- A printed map of the Metro (phones were not smart yet)
- A Spanish phrasebook — actually used it
- Comfortable walking shoes for everyone, including our four-year-old
