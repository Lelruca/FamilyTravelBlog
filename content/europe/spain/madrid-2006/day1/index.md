We landed at Barajas in the early afternoon. The metro from the airport was clean, fast, and mercifully straightforward — four stops to Nuevos Ministerios, a transfer, and we were at Retiro station by 3pm.

The hotel was on a quiet street off the Paseo del Prado. We dropped the bags and immediately walked to the park.

## El Retiro

The Retiro was alive with families on a Friday afternoon. We rented a rowboat on the lake — our daughter was convinced she was steering, and perhaps she was.

{{< photo src="retiro-lake.jpg" size="two-col" alt="Rowing boats on the Retiro lake" caption="The lake at El Retiro, golden hour." >}}

{{< photo src="retiro-path.jpg" size="two-col" caption="One of the long paths through the park." >}}

By the time we left the park it was seven o'clock, which in Madrid is still the afternoon. We found a small bar and had our first glass of Rioja standing up, the way it is meant to be drunk.
