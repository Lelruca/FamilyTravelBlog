Fourteen hours from departure to Shinjuku station. We took the Narita Express, which is exactly as clean and punctual as everyone says.

The hotel was in Shinjuku, which turned out to be the right choice: everything is within twenty minutes on the metro, and at night the neighbourhood is its own spectacle.

We were asleep by nine, which in Tokyo terms meant we had missed the entire evening. We did not care.
