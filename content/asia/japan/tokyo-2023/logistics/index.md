## The IC Card

Get a Suica or Pasmo card at any major station. It works on every metro, most trains, most buses, and in most convenience stores. Top it up with cash at any machine.

## The Shinkansen

We booked all Shinkansen segments in advance through the JR Pass. Whether the JR Pass is worth it depends entirely on your itinerary — for three weeks hitting Tokyo, Hakone, Kyoto, and Hiroshima, it was worth it by a comfortable margin.

## Getting from Narita

The Narita Express (N'EX) is comfortable and reliable. The Limousine Bus is cheaper and goes directly to more hotels but takes longer depending on traffic.
